engineer

Once I have an idea in mind, I love to build prototypes by combining technologies I have at hand and acquiring any new skills necessary on the fly. Consulting knowledgeable people along the way is not only indespensible, but fun too! 

data-phile 

I genuinely love finding patterns in data, extracting knowledge and challenging the conclusions drawn from them. Whether these are patterns in genetics, language or table based data.

my latest project

Conceptualized, prototyped and eventually implemented (JavaScript only) from scratch, the spell colorizer corrects scientific text fully automated based on scientific text pattern recognition.
